SlowTox Visualized

Client HUD for SlowTox intoxication and buffs

Built for the Teotale Vintage Story server (www.teotale.com). Shows blood-alcohol level, a color palette tied to how drunk you are, and a strip of active effects - without opening character stats.

Important - SlowTox is required

Without SlowTox (mods.vintagestory.at/slowtox), SlowTox Visualized has no meaningful role: every status and tooltip is tied to the intoxication rules and stats from that mod. Install SlowTox first - this client HUD only surfaces what SlowTox already computes.

Main HUD

The mug and large number show your intoxication level; colours shift with how drunk you are (SlowTox).

Mug and big number
Big percent readout with palette-driven fill and outline—readable in the corner of the screen.
Example

How it can look in-game at different intoxication levels (layout and colours depend on your settings).

Status strip

A row of small icons for active SlowTox buffs and debuffs (defence, mining, poison, healing, slows, damage boosts, stability, weakness, and more). Hover each icon in-game for a quick tooltip.

Configuration
Optional: edit slowtoxvisualized-hudlayout.json in your Vintage Story ModConfig folder (alongside your worlds). Save the file, then press F9 in-game by default to reload—you’ll get a short chat message when it applies.

Requires the SlowTox mod. Supported game versions are listed on the mod download page.
